Welcome To
Can You Hear Me

A webapp designed for the hard of hearing and differently abled.

Designed with love

An approach to build bridges and make the world a little more accesible for everyone

Committed to bringing a change

This web app was conceived out an idea to do good for the differently abled community. We hope this app helps out and is able to make contributions by making life easier for the hard of hearing and deak community. It is a way of trying to promote inclusivity and creating a safe space for the existance of a thriving community

Our Services

Inclusivity

The presence of an exclusive community for members who use the service. We encourage the creation of safe spaces where people can be themselves without the fear of judgement.

Better Speech to Text

Inclusion of grounbreaking denoising technology that hopes to make your communication more clear and error free. This component actively tries to remove background disturbances in audio that aids in better speech recognition.

Engaging Blogs

A special blog feature that allows members to share what they love and love what others share. Features an interactive comment section for more conversation all around.

Resources/News

A dedicated news section so that you stay up to date with recent improvements and laws pertaining to the community. Features live updates every 5 minutes.
